Resume Writing - From ACK to discard stack: How I learned to stop worrying and hate the resume.


Event
League of Professional System Administrators, East Tennessee
Location
Knoxville, TN, United States

There are no perfect resumes, however, there are plenty of horrible resumes.

Over the years, I’ve become a go-to resource for taking friends’ resumes and covering them with the battle scars of an anal-retentive judge of Oxford commas, mismatched styles, and bewildering capitalization.

This talk covers the obvious, and not so obvious, errors that I encounter in every resume, including my own, that make an instant difference between it being recycled and scheduling an on-site interview.

The talk is interactive with the participants working line-to-line through a sample resume of questionable quality to find the plethora of issues that may be lurking within their own resumes.
